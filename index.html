Anthony's Crystal Collector

Instructions

At the start of the game, there will be a randomly generated number called the target number. By clicking on one of the four crystals you will add a certian amount of points to the total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to generated number and you lose the game if your total score goes above the generated number. You will not be able to see the value of the crystals until you click on one.

Crystal value will vary each game.
